Responsive Design

A design approach that makes a website automatically adjust and adapt its layout, images, and functionality to fit different screen sizes, devices, and orientations (like mobile, tablet, laptop, or desktop).

  • Website layout adjusts automatically to different screen sizes (mobile, tablet, laptop, desktop).
  • Images, videos, and content scale to fit the screen without breaking.
  • Design starts with small screens and scales up for larger devices.
  • Google prefers responsive sites for better search ranking.
